Privacy & Donation Policy

Privacy Policy

Faith Hope Relief respects your privacy. Any personal information collected during donations, subscriptions, or contact forms is kept confidential and used solely for communication and donation management purposes.

Information We Collect:

  • Name, email, phone number (if provided)
  • Donation amount and transaction details
  • Optional messages or comments submitted

How We Use Your Information:

  • To process donations securely
  • To send updates about campaigns and initiatives
  • To maintain transparent records for accountability

Data Security:

All information is stored securely and is not shared with unauthorized third parties. Donor privacy is a top priority.

Donation Policy

Faith Hope Relief ensures that all donations are used responsibly and transparently for humanitarian aid.

Donation Usage:

  • Medical Aid: Life-saving treatments, hospital expenses, medications.
  • Education: School fees, books, and learning materials for children.
  • Disaster Relief: Food, shelter, and emergency assistance for affected families.
  • Community Support: Initiatives that empower local communities.

Transparency & Reporting:

All donations are tracked and documented. Receipts and updates will be shared via the campaign page and email whenever applicable.

Refund Policy:

Donations are voluntary and cannot be refunded. However, we ensure that every contribution is utilized for the intended purpose.
